Our Medical Services

Primary Care

This service provides routine and preventive healthcare to patients, including check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for common illnesses.

Vaccinations

This service offers immunizations against various diseases, such as flu, measles, and HPV, to protect patients from infectious diseases.

Cardiology

This service specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart and blood vessel disorders, including heart attacks, strokes, and high blood pressure.

Genetic Testing

This service involves analyzing a patient's genetic material (DNA or RNA) to identify genetic disorders, predict disease risk, or determine the best course of treatment.

Physical Therapy

This service helps patients recover from injuries or illnesses that affect their physical abilities, such as mobility, strength, and flexibility. Physical therapists use exercises, stretches, and other techniques to promote healing and improve function.

Lab Services

This service provides diagnostic testing and analysis of blood, urine, and other bodily fluids to help healthcare providers diagnose and manage diseases. Lab services may include tests for infections, allergies, and other conditions.
